Concept

Critical reading is when the reader is in an active position to understand the text and question it, this develop the critical thinking. To understand the text you need to analize it, so one recomendation is to read another bibliography to compare and contrast different points of view. This invite us to make judgements about the text, for that the reader need to read in a objective way.
