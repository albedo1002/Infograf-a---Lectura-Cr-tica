Critical Reading

Concept

Critical reading is when the reader is in an active position to understand the text and question it, this develop the critical thinking. To understand the text you need to analize it, so one recomendation is to read another bibliography to compare and contrast different points of view. This invite us to make judgements about the text, for that the reader need to read in a objective way.

Main Features

Critical thinking is a meta-cognitive process that allow us to regule the performance and behavior with the interpretation; this is result of literacy. Critical thinking develop this abilities: memorization, comparation, organize ideas, synthesis, present information; and how you get this? writing different types of text and learning contents of lectures.

When to Use It

Methods and functions... Estrategies: Abstraction (Main idea and function), analize (text study of the components), synthesis (components relation), deduction/comparate (read another bibliography), clasificate (organize data), evaluate (literacy criticism, ex: opinion, valuate).

Importance for professional life

Academic level in the study of the texts: Use of the academic knowledge for analize the quality of the text. This develop the comunication of the text and the reader, for this the reader need to study the texts.
